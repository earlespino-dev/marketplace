## Key durable facts (verify against the PDF before quoting)

- **Course:** ~196–197 miles, Timberline Lodge (Mt. Hood) → Seaside (Pacific coast). 36 legs.
- **Required night safety gear (STRICTLY ENFORCED):** from **6:00pm–7:00am** every runner on
  course must wear a reflective vest + front and back LED flasher + carry a flashlight / wear a
  headlamp or hat light. **Reflective vest must be worn until 9:00am.** Team minimum: 2
  reflective vests, 2 LED flashers, 2 flashlights/headlamps.
- **Local-team volunteer rule:** any team with a member within 100 miles of Portland is "local"
  and must provide **3 volunteers** or be disqualified.
- **Van exchanges:** vans only swap at major exchanges 6, 12, 18, 24, 30. Active van always
  carries 6 racers.
- **Start times:** assigned in a Friday **2:00am–2:00pm** window based on team pace survey.
  Violating your assigned start time = disqualification.
